Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 21, 2026 · Application: GildedPixelCo Pin Scheduler

This Privacy Policy describes how the GildedPixelCo Pin Scheduler application ("the App") handles information. The App is a private tool operated by GildedPixelCo solely to publish the owner's own product content to the owner's own Pinterest account. It is not offered to or used by any third parties.

1. Information the App accesses

The App connects to the Pinterest API using an access token authorized by the account owner. With that authorization, the App can:

  • Read the owner's own Pinterest boards (to know where to publish).
  • Create pins on the owner's own boards, including a title, description, image, and a link to the owner's Etsy product listings.

The App accesses only the owner's own Pinterest account. It does not access, collect, or store data belonging to any other Pinterest user or any website visitor.

2. Information the App does NOT collect

  • No personal data is collected from Pinterest users, Pinners, or website visitors.
  • No browsing data, cookies, analytics, or tracking of any individuals.
  • No data is sold, rented, or shared with third parties.

3. How data is used

The Pinterest access token and the owner's own product images and listing links are used only to create and schedule pins on the owner's Pinterest account. Credentials are stored privately by the owner and are never published or shared.

4. Data retention & security

Access tokens are stored locally and securely by the account owner and can be revoked at any time from the owner's Pinterest account settings, which immediately ends the App's access.

5. Third-party services

The App interacts with the Pinterest API (governed by Pinterest's own terms and privacy policy) and references publicly available Etsy product listing images and URLs.

6. Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated from time to time. The "Last updated" date above reflects the latest revision.

7. Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to info@aiunderstanding.org.
